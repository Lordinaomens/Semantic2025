The Creatives: Alexander McQueen – A Master of Originality and Innovation

In this video, the creator explores the beautiful yet tragic life story of Alexander McQueen, one of the most iconic and influential designers in fashion history. Watch to learn about his journey from London’s Savile Row to his rise at Central Saint Martins, his appointment at a legendary fashion house, and the launch of his own label, 'Alexander McQueen'. This story also covers the challenges McQueen faced with fame and fortune, giving us an insight into his remarkable career and life.
