Studio Overview

My studio is a multidisciplinary creative space where visual storytelling, fashion heritage, and modern design come together. Each service is tailored to help individuals and brands express their unique identity through impactful, meaningful visuals. Whether you’re building a brand, launching a campaign, or just need design guidance — I offer a range of creative services grounded in research, style, and heart.

Creative Services

  • Art Direction: Visual storytelling for campaigns, editorials, and personal brands
  • Fashion History Researcher: Conducting in-depth research into the evolution of fashion through different eras
  • Graphic Design / Information Graphics: Creating impactful visuals for branding, campaigns, and public messaging
  • Textile & Fiber Arts: Designing hand-crafted works using tufting, embroidery, and textile manipulation
  • Fashion Styling: Conceptualizing and curating wardrobe looks for shoots and projects
  • Digital Content Creation: Producing visual assets such as moodboards, lookbooks, and animated slides
