My Creative Journey

Since I was a kid, I’ve loved art — it's been a passion of mine for as long as I can remember. Whether I was doodling in the margins of my notebooks or crafting clothes for my dolls, creativity has always been my way of connecting with the world.

Where It All Started

My journey into the creative world began with simple drawings and a curiosity for color. Over the years, that spark grew into a deep love for fashion, visual storytelling, and design. I was especially inspired by how art can be both expressive and functional — telling stories, honoring culture, and making bold statements.

Growing Through Experience

From school projects to independent passion work, I’ve explored a wide range of mediums — from illustration and textiles to digital design. Every step has taught me something new, and every piece has been a chance to refine my voice as a designer.

What Keeps Me Going

What drives me today is the belief that art and design have the power to shape narratives, inspire change, and bring people together. I want my work to not only reflect my personal journey but also uplift the stories and identities of others through bold visuals and thoughtful design.
